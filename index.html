How to Use

The below buttons at the top of the field add features (players, cones, balls, notes) to the drill.
To have a player run, hold Ctrl and click and drag the mouse to the player's destination.
To have a ball move, hold Ctrl and click and drag the mouse to the ball's destination.

Click on a player/ball/cone to edit or delete it.
Click on a note, or the beginning of a pass or run, in the timeline to edit or delete it.

Scroll through the timeline to advance time.
At the beginning of the timeline, you can drag things around, if you've advanced time you can only add movements (runs or passes).
